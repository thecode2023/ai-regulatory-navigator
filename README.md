# AI Regulatory Navigator

> Which AI regulations apply to your company? Find out in 30 seconds.

🔴 **Live App:** 

```markdown
https://ai-regulatory-navigator.vercel.app
```

Companies deploying AI agents internationally face a fragmented regulatory landscape — EU AI Act, US state laws, APAC frameworks, and more. This tool maps applicable regulations based on your jurisdictions, industry, and AI use cases.

## What It Does

- Input where you operate, your industry, and how you use AI
- Get mapped regulations across 15+ jurisdictions
- See risk classifications per jurisdiction
- Generate a compliance checklist and roadmap
- Export a board-ready summary

## Built With

- **Claude Code** — entire app built from terminal
- **Manus** — autonomous regulatory research across jurisdictions
- **Claude API** — regulatory analysis and recommendations
- **Vercel** — deployment
